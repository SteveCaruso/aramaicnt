---

**Codex Climaci Rescriptus** is a palimpsest — a manuscript that was written, had its original text scratched off (because parchment was expensive), and then had another text written on top of it.

The underlayer, in this case, are portions of the Old and New Testaments in Christian Palestinian Aramaic, made roughly around the 5th century.
